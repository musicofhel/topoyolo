Domains

TDA

Topological Data Analysis. Multi-scale filtration; the only domain where persistence diagrams are native objects.

QEC

Quantum Error Correction. Finite fields; error thresholds as phase transitions. The richest chain complex instantiation (16+ papers).

Dynamical Systems

Temporal structure, delay embedding, attractor reconstruction from partial observation. Takens embedding has no QEC analogue.

Neuroscience

Population-level topology. Both undirected co-firing complexes and directed synaptic complexes. Where the chain complex bifurcates.

Information Theory

The home of joint-vs-marginal excess. PID decomposition, information cohomology, Shannon entropy as 1-cocycle.

Chain Complex

The prototypical shared structure: boundary operators satisfying ∂²=0, from simplicial complexes in TDA to code complexes in QEC to Möbius functions on information-theoretic posets. Seven incarnations documented: geometric, algebraic, categorified, combinatorial, gauge-theoretic, graded-informational, and neural (with directed and undirected sub-flavors).

Structure in composite systems that is absent from components. Binding residuals in TDA, entanglement in QEC, emergent attractor topology in dynamics, neural binding and consciousness (Φ) in neuroscience, mutual information and synergy in information theory. Anti-Synthesis

The goal is not synthesis or unification. It is differentiation — in the Deleuzian sense. The same abstract machine is actualized differently in each domain. By cataloguing the repetitions and the genuine divergences, we make visible the plane that connects them.

The domains diverge in ways that matter:

  • QEC operates over finite fields (ℤ/2); persistent homology typically over the reals. Same machine, different arithmetic, different answers.
  • Takens delay embedding reconstructs a manifold from a single observable. QEC starts with the complex already given. The shared structure begins only after construction.
  • Code distance is a single number; a persistence diagram encodes the full birth-death spectrum. Related but structurally different objects.
  • Tsuda's attractor ruins — destroyed attractors that retain geometric influence — have no persistence bar and no error-correction analogue. A sixth flavor of stability.

The divergences are as informative as the repetitions. ANTISYNONYMS.md tracks all 30.

Methodology

Each paper is annotated along four axes: which abstract machine it instantiates, its domain-specific vocabulary mapped to shared terms, what is genuinely new (structure that does not reduce to the shared abstraction), and whether the paper acknowledges the cross-domain parallel or is blind to it.

Papers are dual-indexed under both by-domain/ and by-structure/. The same paper appears in two places, revealing which disciplinary silo it sits in and which abstract machine it instantiates. The cross-references are the point.
